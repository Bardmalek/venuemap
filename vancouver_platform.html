How it works

From idea to booked
in under 5 minutes.

No spreadsheets, no back-and-forth emails, no chasing permits. Just pick, pay, and show up.

01
Find your event
Browse upcoming festivals, markets, and cultural events across Metro Vancouver filtered by date, location, and booth type.
02
Pick your spot
View the real venue floor plan on satellite imagery. Click any available booth to see its exact size, price, and amenities.
03
Apply & pay
Submit business details, documents, and a server-verified booking hold. Generic PayPal capture is staged as the next payment pass.
04
Show up & sell
Receive a vendor package with receipt, permit state, and event-day credentials once payment and review are complete.
