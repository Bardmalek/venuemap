How it works

From idea to booked
in under 5 minutes.

No spreadsheets, no back-and-forth emails, no chasing permits. Just pick, pay, and show up.

01
🗺️
Find your event
Browse upcoming festivals, markets, and cultural events across Metro Vancouver filtered by date, location, and booth type.
02
📍
Pick your spot
View the real venue floor plan on satellite imagery. Click any available booth to see its exact size, price, and amenities.
03
✍️
Apply & pay
Fill in your business details, upload required documents, sign your contract digitally, and pay securely via Stripe.
04
✅
Show up & sell
Receive your QR code confirmation. Scan in on event day and you're ready. Permits handled automatically.
